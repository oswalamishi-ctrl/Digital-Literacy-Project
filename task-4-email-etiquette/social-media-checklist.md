✅ Do’s
✔️ Think before you post anything online
✔️ Keep your personal information private
✔️ Use strong and secure passwords
✔️ Be respectful and polite in comments and messages
✔️ Verify information before sharing it
❌ Don’ts
❌ Don’t share passwords or OTPs with anyone
❌ Don’t post inappropriate or offensive content
❌ Don’t click on suspicious links
❌ Don’t spread fake news or rumors
❌ Don’t overshare personal details (like address, phone number)
